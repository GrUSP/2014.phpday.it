We have already received more than 150 proposals but we want more!

Remember that the CFP will end on Feb 28th, so hurry up and don’t miss the chance to be part of phpDay 2014! Please remember that the phpDay is an international conference, so session proposals must be in English.

Tickets are available, get one at the discounted early bird price until they last!

Thanks to our first sponsors: Aruba with a Diamond level; Ideato, JetBrains and SensioLabsInsight with a Platinum level, Windows Azure a gold sponsor and ServerGrove as bronze. Do you want to be part of phpDay 2014 as a sponsor? Take a look to our sponsorship options!
